School Campus Overview

About Gundlav Primary School

  • Established in 1955 and managed by a Local Body.
  • Located in a Rural area within the Valsad block of Valsad district, Gujarat.
  • Offers education for Grade Balvatika & 1 to 8.
  • Gujarati is the medium of instruction.
  • Academic session commences in June.
  • Operates from a Government building with 19 functional classrooms.
  • Equipped with functional tap water and toilets.
  • Includes a dedicated playground and a library.
